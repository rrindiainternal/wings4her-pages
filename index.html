About Wings4Her app

Wings4Her is an app that serves as a resource hub for aspiring students, helping them find various paths to successful STEM careers. The app provides them access to a STEM-focused aptitude test, career counselling guides, parental counselling guides, scholarship resources, live webinars, shareable videos, FAQs, and more. It is dedicated to bridging the gender gap and enhancing female representation in STEM fields.

This app aims to inspire and engage young female students who wish to pursue careers in STEM subjects, thereby stimulating innovation and creating new opportunities for growth and development.
